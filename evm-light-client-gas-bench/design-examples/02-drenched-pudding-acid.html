Slide 02 · The gap

The cost lives in signatures, not Merkle proofs.

CometBFT signs ed25519 over per-signer canonical-vote bytes. Each signer's message includes its own timestamp.

Solidity has no ed25519 precompile, and per-signer messages defeat BLS aggregation. The light client either keeps the message shape and pays linearly, or asks CometBFT to change protocol.
