Slide 02 · The gap

Five schemes line up against one wall: the EVM doesn't speak ed25519.

CometBFT signs ed25519 over per-signer canonical-vote bytes. Solidity has no ed25519 precompile, and per-signer messages defeat aggregation.

Each scheme answers the same wall differently. Four cost less than secp on raw gas — three of those four require CometBFT to start signing one shared precommit message per committed block.
